THE PLAYER

Lord of Salt

Upon moving to Sault Saint Marie about a year and a half ago, Todd was introduce to Magic The Gathering by an old friend. With almost no experience with card games Todd spent his early days picking up the basics of the game. He quick moved on to building better and more competitive decks. Influence by those he played with his preferred format is modern and his first real deck was based on the Infect archetype. Todd try's to play the game as often as he can and enjoys improving his skills and tries his best to avoid miss-plays while attempting to not get overly salty.
