About the Band

Fleshgod Apocalypse was formed in April 2007, they have release 3 full length studio albums and are currently working on a fouth album entitled "King". Fleshgod Apocalypse is is an Italian technical death metal band.

Current Line-up

  • Cristiano Trionfera – lead guitar, backing vocals (2007–present)
  • Paolo Rossi – bass guitar, clean vocals (2007–present)
  • Francesco Paoli – drums, backing vocals (2009–present)
  • Tommaso Riccardi – lead vocals, rhythm guitar (2009–present)
  • Francesco Ferrini – piano, string arrangements, orchestral effects (2010–present)

Albums

  • Oracles (2009)
  • Agony (2011)
  • Labyrinth (2013)
  • King (2016)
