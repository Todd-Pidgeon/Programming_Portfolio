Terminator 2: Judgement Day

Terminator 2: Judgement Day is, in my opinion, the greatest science fiction action movie of all time. It also happens to be one of the greatest sequels of all time. The original Terminator movie was great in it's own right, however I feel through the success of the first film, James Cameron was able to have the budget needed to make Terminator 2 the movie he had always envisioned and that it deserved to be. Terminator 2: Judgement Day holds up as an outstanding achievement in special effects, still looking great and believable to this day. The film also cemented the Terminator as a character in all our minds and will continue as one of the great action icons for all time.

Main Actors

  • Arnold Schwarzenegger
  • Linda Hamilton
  • Edward Furlong
  • Robert Patrick
