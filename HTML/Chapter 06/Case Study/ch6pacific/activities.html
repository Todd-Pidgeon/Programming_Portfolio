Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trail and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guests use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific TRails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.
